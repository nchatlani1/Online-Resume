Project information

  • Title: Calorie Tracker Application
  • Class: AP Computer Science
  • Project date: May 20, 2020
  • Project URL: link to APK file

In my junior year of high school (2019), I took AP Computer Science. In this class, our final project was to create an application of choice. As a fitness oriented individual, I created a calorie tracking app with various databases used to track individual daily caloric intake databases which could look at the caloric intake from a broader view, showing all of the tracked days. The program also had the ability to save commonly eaten foods in databases so you didn't have to type in a food you had more than once. After taking the AP Test, I recieved college credit for GE Computer Science Coursework.
